Assistant Professor

Pomona College

I am an assistant professor at Pomona College in Claremont, CA, where I teach in the Psychological Science and Neuroscience Departments. I also lead the Mechanisms of Interpersonal Communication research lab (MICLab) where we study the psychological, neural, and behavioral mechanisms that support interpersonal communication and coordination.
